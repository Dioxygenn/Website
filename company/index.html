About Us

Atailda is a company that believes in making the web a better place. We believe that everyone should be able to access the best products and services, regardless of their location or ability to pay.

That's why we've created Armonium: an app that helps you manage your stress levels before exams, so you can do your best on test day. To make our app available to as many people as possible, we've made it free and accessible to anyone with an internet connection.

We care about privacy too—we don't ask you for any personal information when you download our app, and we don't store any of your data after use. We're two best friends who decided to create an app together because we wanted something more for ourselves and other people like us. We hope that someday soon there will be no need for Atailda because everyone will live in a world where access to good health care is easy and affordable—but until then, we'll keep working hard to make sure our service is available everywhere it needs to be.
